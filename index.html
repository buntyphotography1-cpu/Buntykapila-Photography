Behind the lens

Meet Bunty Kapila

For more than six years, Bunty and his professional team have captured the moments people never want to forget—from the quiet anticipation of maternity to lively Punjabi celebrations and intimate family milestones.

Based in Pakenham, we combine thoughtful direction, genuine emotion and cinematic editing to create photographs and films that feel personal rather than posed. Our style is warm, polished and designed to remain timeless long after the celebration ends.
